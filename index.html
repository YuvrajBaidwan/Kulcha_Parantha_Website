The foundation

The Ingredients Behind the Craft

The process begins with ingredients chosen for flavour, texture and memory — each one playing its part before the first roll, the first char, and the final brush of ghee.

Stone-Ground Atta

Whole wheat milled daily on traditional chakki stones for that earthy aroma and protein-rich texture.

Pure Ghee

Clarified butter from grass-fed cows, slow-simmered for six hours until it turns liquid gold.

Fresh Herbs

Coriander, mint & fenugreek picked before sunrise — the green soul of every stuffing.

Hand-Picked Spices

Cumin, ajwain & fennel toasted to order — each batch ground in small quantities for peak flavour.

Heritage Fillings

Paneer, spiced potato & black lentil — recipes passed down through four generations of home kitchens.

Clay Oven Fire

Traditional tandoor fired with mango wood at 650°C — the intense heat creates that signature char and airy crumb.
