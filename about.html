Our Story

The Journey That
Brought Us Here

Every great brand starts with a simple idea. Ours started with a belief — that Indian comfort food deserves the same love, experience, and identity as any global cuisine.

Founded by young entrepreneur Rahul Tyagi, the journey began from a humble momo cart — driven by passion, consistency, and the dream of building something bigger. What started small soon transformed into a growing food ecosystem with the success of Dimsum Box, now proudly serving customers through 100+ outlets across India.

But while building successful food brands, one thing became clear — the new generation was evolving. They wanted authentic flavours, but they also wanted aesthetics, hygiene, experience, and spaces they could connect with.

That vision led to the creation of Kulcha & Prantha Co. — a modern Indian food brand built to redefine the way people experience traditional comfort food. Where buttery kulchas, loaded pranthas, and handcrafted beverages meet an atmosphere that feels urban, vibrant, and contemporary.

Our Vision

Redefining Indian Comfort
Food for a New Generation

We aim to build a brand where traditional Indian flavours meet contemporary experiences — creating spaces that are vibrant, hygienic, aesthetic, and deeply rooted in Indian culture. Our goal is to transform everyday dishes like kulchas and pranthas into experiences people connect with, celebrate, and come back to.

We envision Kulcha & Prantha Co. becoming a nationwide symbol of modern Indian dining — where authenticity, quality, and innovation exist together. Because we believe the future of food is not about replacing tradition. It is about reinventing it for a new generation.

Our Journey

From a Cart to 100+ Outlets

2017

The Humble Beginning

Rahul Tyagi starts with a small momo cart in Chandigarh. No investors. No backup. Just a belief in the product and an obsession with quality.

2019

Dimsum Box Is Born

The first Dimsum Box outlet opens. Within months, the brand develops a cult following — proving that affordable, quality Asian street food has a massive untapped market in India.

2021

Rapid Expansion

Dimsum Box crosses 30 outlets across North India. The FOFO (Franchise-Owned, Franchise-Operated) model proves itself as a sustainable, high-quality growth engine.

2023

A New Vision Takes Shape

Seeing the gap in the Indian flatbread space, the team begins crafting the concept for Kulcha & Prantha Co. — a brand built to bring traditional Indian comfort food into the modern era.

2024

Kulcha & Prantha Co. Launches

The first outlet opens in Chandigarh to an overwhelming response. The menu — built around customisable paranthas, tandoori kulchas, and a vibrant dining experience — strikes an immediate chord.

2025

100+ & Growing

With 100+ outlets across India and franchise queries pouring in from new cities, the brand enters its next chapter — scaling without compromising the quality that made it successful.
