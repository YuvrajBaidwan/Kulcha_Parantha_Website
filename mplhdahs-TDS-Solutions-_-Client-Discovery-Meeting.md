# **Business Overview**

## **What does the business do?**

* **Kulcha & Prantha Co.** is a modern Indian QSR (Quick Service Restaurant) brand that serves authentic Indian comfort food in a clean, urban, and Gen Z-inspired atmosphere.  
* The brand specializes in a wide variety of **kulchas, stuffed pranthas, Indian street-style sides, beverages, and fusion comfort meals**, combining traditional Indian flavors with a contemporary dining experience. Our focus is on delivering high-quality food, hygienic preparation, innovative presentation, and aesthetically designed spaces that connect with today’s generation.  
* Founded by entrepreneur **Rahul Tyagi**, the brand is built with the vision of redefining Indian comfort food and creating a nationwide food culture around modern Indian dining.

## **Target audience/customers**

Kulcha & Prantha Co. is built for the modern Indian customer who wants tasty, filling, and good-quality food at an affordable price.

Our primary audience includes:

* College students  
* Young working professionals  
* Families  
* Food lovers looking for quick and satisfying meals  
* Gen Z customers who enjoy aesthetic and trendy food spaces

The brand focuses on serving value-for-money meals that are flavorful, hygienic, and pocket-friendly — where customers can enjoy a complete and fulfilling dining experience without spending excessively.

At Kulcha & Prantha Co., we believe great food should be accessible, comforting, and enjoyable for everyone.

## **Main goal of the website**

The primary goal of the Kulcha & Prantha Co. website is to build a strong online presence and attract potential franchise partners across India.

The website is designed to:

* Generate franchise leads  
* Showcase the brand story, vision, and business growth  
* Build trust and credibility among potential investors and clients  
* Present the brand’s modern concept, menu, interiors, and business model  
* Create awareness and visibility for the brand online  
* Serve as a professional portfolio of the company and its food ecosystem

The website will act as a digital platform where customers, investors, and franchise buyers can explore the brand and connect with the company easily.

# Additional Notes

OUR STORY

Every great brand starts with a simple idea.

Ours started with a belief — that Indian comfort food deserves the same love, experience, and identity as any global cuisine.

Founded by young entrepreneur Rahul Tyagi, the journey began from a humble momo cart driven by passion, consistency, and the dream of building something bigger. What started small soon transformed into a growing food ecosystem with the success of Dimsum Box, now proudly serving customers through 100+ outlets across India.

But while building successful food brands, one thing became clear — the new generation was evolving. They wanted authentic flavors, but they also wanted aesthetics, hygiene, experience, and spaces they could connect with.

That vision led to the creation of Kulcha & Prantha Co.

A modern Indian food brand built to redefine the way people experience traditional comfort food. We wanted to create a place where buttery kulchas, loaded pranthas, handcrafted beverages, and rich Indian flavors could be enjoyed in an atmosphere that feels urban, vibrant, and contemporary.

At Kulcha & Prantha Co., we blend the soul of Indian street food with a clean and premium dining experience inspired by today’s culture. Every detail — from our recipes to our interiors — is designed to celebrate India in a fresh and modern way.

More than just a restaurant, we are building a community around food, culture, conversations, and experiences.

Because for us, kulchas and pranthas are not just dishes.

They are emotions, memories, and a part of who we are.

Welcome to Kulcha & Prantha Co.

Where Indian comfort food meets the spirit of a new generation.

OUR VISION 

At Kulcha & Prantha Co., our vision is to redefine Indian comfort food for the modern generation.

We aim to build a brand where traditional Indian flavors meet contemporary experiences — creating spaces that are vibrant, hygienic, aesthetic, and deeply rooted in Indian culture. Our goal is to transform everyday dishes like kulchas and pranthas into experiences people connect with, celebrate, and come back to.

We envision Kulcha & Prantha Co. becoming a nationwide symbol of modern Indian dining — where authenticity, quality, and innovation exist together. Through thoughtfully crafted food, urban-inspired interiors, and a strong community-driven culture, we aspire to make Indian food feel youthful, premium, and globally relevant.

More than serving meals, we want to create moments, conversations, and memories around the food India has always loved.

Because we believe the future of food is not about replacing tradition —

It is about reinventing it for a new generation.
